Free Civilisation Project

The goal of the Free Civilisation Project is to provide the humankind with free and open source technologies and philosophy necessary to create and maintain a complete modern human civilisation.

Why

Human civilisation of the future has to be shaped in a way that makes each individual free and equal. The basic principle of a human civilisation must be the utmost respect for these basic values: Life, freedom, safety, dignity. The Free Civilisation Project's technology and philosophy is to be architected from the ground up to adhere to this principle.
